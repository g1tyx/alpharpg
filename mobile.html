Introduction

Welcome to AlphaRPG, a game made by Purple Wizard.
The main goal of this game is to fight enemies and progress through the story to gain EQUIPMENTS and EXPERIENCE.
You will discover a new world named Alpha with various kingdoms & monsters.
But be careful there are also powerful monsters named BOSS so you need to fight wisely to deal with them.
Also, if you have any question, don't hesitate to join our discord server.
